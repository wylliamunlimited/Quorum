# AUTH-103 — Beta users must not lose their accounts

**Type:** User Story
**Status:** To Do
**Priority:** High

## The want
> As a beta user who's been here for months, I want to keep my existing account
> after the login update, so that I don't have to start over or wonder if my
> stuff is gone.

These are our earliest, most loyal users. Making them re-register — or worse,
silently losing their accounts — is exactly how we'd lose the people who
believed in us first.

## What "done" looks like (user outcomes)
- Everyone who already has an account can still log in afterward, same as before.
- **Nobody's account or data disappears** during the update.
- Context for the team: there are roughly **4,000 real beta accounts** live
  today. Losing any of them is a **launch-blocker**, not a cleanup detail.
